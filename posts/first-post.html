Hello, World

2026-01-21

This is it. My own corner of the internet.

I've been building things for a while now, scattered across repositories and forgotten folders. This site is my attempt to bring them together in one place, and more importantly, to start writing about the process.

What you'll find here

Projects: Everything I've built that I think is worth sharing. Code, experiments, tools. Some finished, some perpetually in progress.

Writing: Thoughts on building things, lessons learned, and whatever else I find interesting enough to put into words.

Why now

I've always believed that the best way to learn is to build, and the best way to solidify learning is to explain it. This blog is the explaining part.

Plus, there's something satisfying about having your own domain. spiffler.xyz is mine.
